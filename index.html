Tesla: The German Shepherd Mix

Tesla's Story

Tesla is a German Shepherd Mix that was born in Ireland in 2015. Tesla did not have a home in Ireland so an organization called Hundar Utan Hem brought her to Sweden. In Sweden, she was adopted by her new family. Tesla graduated from Puppy Training School in 2016. Tesla now works as a family pet and is very happy to have a loving home.
